Golf Handicap Calculator

Enter up to your last 20 rounds

Need a minimum of 54 holes to start calculating your handicap

That can be either 3 rounds of 18 or a combination of 9 holes (paired together) until 54 holes are met

NOTE: if using 9 hole rounds those rounds will ONLY count when 2 rounds of 9 are paired together.

Example: If you have 4 rounds of 9 you will have 2 sets of '18 hole scores' which will count as 2 rounds of golf. If you have 3 rounds of 9 you will have 1 set of 18 hole scores with that remaining 9 hole round waiting until another round of 9 is entered. Once another round of 9 is entered it gets paired with the left over round of 9

NOTE: These calculations are based on the WHS (World Handicap System) pre 2024 change.
